# THE SLAVE DANCER - Quick Reference Card

## YOUR THREE STATS
| Stat | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Survival** | Physical endurance; can you keep living? |
| **Humanity** | Your compassion and integrity; can you stay yourself? |
| **Resolve** | Mental strength; can you keep choosing what's right? |

---

## EVERY TURN (5 Steps)

1. **Roll** the die
2. **Move** that many spaces forward
3. **Read** the event at that space
4. **Choose** one of 2–3 options (all are always available)
5. **Apply** the stat changes shown + check for state consequences

---

## STAT CHANGES

**Normal choice** → Apply changes shown  
**Risky choice (⚠)** → May fail based on your stats

**Failure chance = 60% − (Your Stat × 3%)**, minimum 10%  
Example: If your Survival is 8, failure chance = 60 − 24 = **36%**

If you fail:
- Lose 1 point from a related stat
- Lose the gains you would have had

---

## STATE CONSEQUENCES (After Each Turn)

✓ If **Humanity ≤ 3** → Lose 1 Resolve (guilt weighs on you)  
✓ If **Resolve ≥ 8** → Gain 1 Survival (strong will helps you live)

---

## WINNING THE GAME

**Goal:** First to complete 1 full lap (traverse spaces 1–20 and return)  
**Winner:** Whoever has the highest **Survival + Humanity + Resolve**

### Example Final Scores
| Player | Survival | Humanity | Resolve | **Total** |
|--------|----------|----------|---------|-----------|
| Maya | 12 | 8 | 11 | **31** ← Wins |
| James | 14 | 6 | 9 | 29 |

---

## REMEMBER

✓ No choice is "correct"—only consequences  
✓ Your past decisions affect your future chances (through your stats)  
✓ Low stats make risky choices more likely to fail  
✓ You can always choose any option  
✓ The winner is who survives **and** retains the most of themselves
